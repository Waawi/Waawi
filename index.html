About LOST AMBER
Fight for Life, trust your destiny.

Genre

Action RPG

Style

2D - Top down

Platforms

PC - Consoles

Status

In development

Lost Amber is the first title from Waawi Studio, a 2D top down action role-playing game, with a dark cute aesthetic.

As a castaway in an hostile world, you stumble upon a mysterious Amber, that holds an amazing power.

Explore the world, evolve your character, choose your fighting style, accomplish your quest !

Team

Cyrille Lagarigue

He likes retro gaming, single player Starcraft, loves platform games, action/acrade games and Dark Souls! Programmer and designer.

He worked at Ubisoft on hi-profile games (Assassin's Creed 2-3), and other projects like a game that cures an eye sickness.
Then he went indie, shipped Streets of Fury EX on Steam, and started working on Lost Amber.
